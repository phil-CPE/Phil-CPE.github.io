Project Thesis

FPGA-based predictive maintenance system for Permanent Split Capacitor (PSC) motors. We utilize vibration and current monitoring to detect early-stage hardware fatigue.

Hardware Outcome

By synchronizing three independent ESP32 nodes, the system compares energy consumption gaps and provides thermal thresholds for industrial hardware safety.
